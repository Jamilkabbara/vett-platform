# Pass 23 B10 — Product Hunt Launch Checklist

**Owner:** Jamil (manual execution)
**Generated:** 2026-04-29
**Pass 23 phase:** B10 (Phase B — Grow)

This is the operational checklist for VETT's Product Hunt launch. Product
Hunt is a high-leverage but narrow launch channel: 1 day of intense
attention, then a long-tail SEO + backlink trail. Best results come from
30 days of pre-launch preparation, then 24 hours of all-hands push on
launch day, then a 2-week follow-through.

---

## T-30 to T-7 — Pre-launch foundation

### Hunter following

**Goal:** 100+ followers on the @VettIt PH account before launch.

- [ ] Create / verify @VettIt account at `producthunt.com`
- [ ] Add bio, profile image, cover, link to vettit.ai
- [ ] Comment thoughtfully on 5-10 launches per week in the AI / market-
      research / B2B SaaS categories. Quality > quantity. Build a face.
- [ ] Follow + occasionally engage with: makers in adjacent categories
      (Pollfish, Typeform, Tally, Maze, Userpilot, Kraftful)
- [ ] Find a "hunter" — a high-rep PH user willing to officially submit
      VETT. Optional but valuable. Reach out 2 weeks ahead.

### Commenter list

**Goal:** 15-20 real people from Jamil's network primed to comment in
the first 6 hours.

- [ ] Compile list of 30 potential commenters (founders, customers,
      LinkedIn network, Twitter follows)
- [ ] DM each 7 days before launch with: launch date + time + what
      a quality comment looks like
- [ ] Send a reminder DM the night before
- [ ] Don't ask for upvotes. PH detects coordinated upvoting and
      shadow-penalizes. Comments + organic engagement do the work.

### Assets

- [ ] **Tagline (60 chars max):** "AI market research in minutes, not
      weeks. From $9."
- [ ] **Description (260 chars max):** "VETT generates synthetic
      respondent personas to your audience spec, simulates surveys,
      and delivers research-grade insights — no panel recruitment,
      pay per mission from $9."
- [ ] **Topic categorization:** AI, Market Research, Survey
- [ ] **Gallery (8-10 images, 1270×760):**
  1. Hero shot — landing page above the fold
  2. Mission Setup wizard
  3. Slider tier markers + Most Popular badge
  4. Results page with executive summary + charts
  5. Cross-cut analysis (segment breakdown)
  6. Creative attention page (4-card scorecard)
  7. Tabbed pricing teaser
  8. NotificationBell with badge (the always-on UX detail)
- [ ] **30-second demo video:** brief description → AI generates
      personas → simulates → report. End on the price card.
- [ ] **Logo:** existing `apple-touch-icon.png` (works for PH thumbnail)
- [ ] **First-comment maker post:** drafted below.

### Schedule

- [ ] **Choose a Tuesday** (best for B2B; Sat-Sun underperform).
- [ ] **00:01 PST launch time** — Product Hunt's clock resets at
      midnight PST. Earlier = more daylight in PST, EST, GMT, GST.
- [ ] **Avoid US holidays** + weeks where another major AI tool is
      launching (check the upcoming-launches feed).

## Launch day — T-0

### 00:01 PST (08:01 GST)

- [ ] Submit launch
- [ ] Pin the maker comment immediately
- [ ] Tweet the launch URL from VETT + Jamil's personal Twitter
- [ ] Post on LinkedIn (Jamil's profile + VETT page)
- [ ] DM the 30 primed commenters
- [ ] Email the existing user base (subject: "We're on Product Hunt today")

### 00:01–04:00 PST — first 4 hours

This window decides everything. Top-of-day rank is sticky.

- [ ] Reply to **every** comment within 30 min
- [ ] Repost on every social channel + Slack/Discord communities
      Jamil belongs to
- [ ] Update Twitter / LinkedIn pinned posts to point to the PH
      launch
- [ ] Post in Indie Hackers, r/SideProject, r/Entrepreneur if
      Jamil has karma there

### 04:00–10:00 PST — MENA timezone advantage

- [ ] By the time US wakes up, MENA + Europe should already have
      pushed VETT into the top 5. Use the timezone window.
- [ ] Reach out to MENA tech media (Wamda, StepFeed) with a
      "MENA founder breaks into Product Hunt top 5" angle if it's
      tracking that way.

### 10:00–18:00 PST — US push

- [ ] Send a second wave of network DMs (people who slept through
      the first one)
- [ ] Reply to every new comment
- [ ] If trending in top 3, re-share with "we're top 3!" framing
- [ ] If outside top 5 by 14:00 PST, accept the result and
      pivot to the post-launch capture (don't burn extra effort
      trying to claw up; the flywheel doesn't work)

### 18:00–24:00 PST — wind-down + capture

- [ ] Final reply pass on all comments
- [ ] Collect all UTM-tagged traffic from the launch into a
      dashboard
- [ ] Tag every signup via PH UTM in funnel_events
- [ ] Get screenshot of final ranking for the recap blog

## T+1 to T+14 — post-launch capture

### Day 1 (T+1)

- [ ] Recap blog post: "What we learned launching on Product Hunt"
      (regardless of ranking — even a #15 finish makes useful
      content)
- [ ] Email subscribers with the recap
- [ ] Update LinkedIn + Twitter profile to "Featured on Product Hunt"

### Week 1 (T+1 to T+7)

- [ ] Apply to PH Daily Digest (auto-applies from a top-5 finish)
- [ ] Cross-post the launch into BetaList for ongoing exposure
- [ ] Submit to Hacker News Show HN (separate launch — different
      audience, don't pre-coordinate; let it run organically)
- [ ] Reach out to anyone who upvoted but didn't comment — invite
      them to be a customer or feedback partner
- [ ] Track week 1 conversion: PH UTM signups → paid missions

### Week 2 (T+8 to T+14)

- [ ] Submit to PH Best of [Month] for monthly digest exposure
- [ ] Republish the recap on Medium / Indie Hackers / dev.to
- [ ] Reach out to anyone who left negative feedback — convert
      to product feedback, fix what's fixable, surface the rest

## Success metrics

| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| PH ranking final | Top 5 for the day |
| New signups via PH UTM | 200+ in launch week |
| Paid missions via PH | 20+ in launch week |
| Backlinks from launch | 30+ (recap blog, news mentions, alt-comparison directories) |
| @VettIt followers | +50 net during launch week |

Anything in the **Top 10** is a successful launch. Top 3 is great. #1 is
a months-long compounding effect — but don't optimize for #1 at the cost
of authentic engagement. The community detects gaming.

---

## Anti-patterns (don't do these)

- ❌ Buying upvotes — PH detects this and shadow-penalizes (worst case: account ban)
- ❌ Asking your network to upvote without commenting — flagged as suspicious
- ❌ Cross-posting the same canned message to every commenter
- ❌ Going dark mid-launch — replies tail off, ranking slides
- ❌ Treating ranking as the only metric — the launch is also a
  brand asset (badge, recap content, backlinks) regardless of rank

## Pre-launch dry-run

7 days before launch, run the launch flow on a dummy submission to
verify:
- [ ] Image gallery uploads + renders correctly
- [ ] Tagline + description fit within character limits
- [ ] All assets have alt text
- [ ] Demo video plays end-to-end without buffering
- [ ] CTA buttons link to the right URL with UTM params
