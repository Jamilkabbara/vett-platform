## 2. What VETT does

VETT is an AI-powered market research platform. You define an audience and a research goal; we generate synthetic respondent personas and simulate the survey, returning structured insights, charts, and a written summary.

VETT supports several mission types ("Goals"):

- **Validate** — concept and product-market-fit testing
- **Brand Lift** — pre/post awareness, recall, attribution measurement
- **Creative Attention** — emotion + attention analysis on uploaded creatives
- **Pricing Research** — willingness-to-pay and sensitivity testing
- **Audience Profiling** — segment definition and persona discovery
- **Naming & Messaging** — concept testing on names, taglines, and copy
- **Market Entry** — readiness assessment for a new market or category
- **Churn Research** — retention drivers and exit signals
- **Customer Satisfaction** — CSAT / NPS-style measurement
- **Compare Concepts** — A/B testing of two or more concepts
- **Test Marketing/Ads** — creative testing for paid media
- **Feature Roadmap** — prioritisation against synthetic user demand
- **General Research** — open-ended custom studies
- **Competitor Analysis** — positioning relative to named competitors

Pricing is goal-keyed and shown on the relevant signup pages at the time of purchase.

## 3. The delivery contract — "10 = 10 qualified"

We make a specific, narrow promise: **the number of respondents you pay for is the number of qualified respondents we deliver.**

Concretely:

- If you order 50 respondents, we generate 50 synthetic respondents who satisfy your screening criteria.
- We do not generate more and filter down. We do not generate fewer and refund proportionally. We use constraint-based generation to produce respondents that meet your spec on the first try.
- If we cannot deliver the qualified number for a reason within our control (AI error, infrastructure failure, parse fault), we refund you in full and surface the failure clearly on the results page. See Section 7 for refunds.

We do not promise:

- That synthetic respondents perfectly mirror real-human distributions.
- That insights from VETT are interchangeable with insights from a real-human research panel.
- That any specific business outcome will follow from a recommendation in a VETT report.

We strongly recommend using VETT for early-stage validation, iteration, and decision support, and validating the highest-stakes calls with primary research before launch.
